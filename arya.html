About Us

Welcome to Arya Bal Bharti Public School

Arya Bal Bharti Public School is one of the most prestigious school in India. Founded as a Public School in 1972 in Panipat, it is a private institution run by the Arya Pratinidhi Sabha Rohtak, Haryana. Arya Bal Bharti Public School is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), which is the largest educational board in the country. It is recognized by the Department of Education, Govt. of India. Over 5000 schools in India, with over 80,000 students, are members of the Board.

Our Culture

In the portals of Arya Bal Bharti Public School students discover their own talents, and get an opportunity to develop them to the fullest. The school provides an invigorating and competitive atmosphere, created by excellent facilities and guidance provided by a highly qualified and dedicated faculty. The values, which are ingrained help to promote confidence, direction, and critical thinking skills, leading to the development of well-adjusted, adaptable and integrated personalities. In other words, ARYA BAL BHARTI PUBLIC SCHOOL offers comprehensive and holistic education.
